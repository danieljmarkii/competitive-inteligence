## SECTION 1: COMPANY SNAPSHOT

**Company**
- Quantum Workplace (QW)
- Website: https://www.quantumworkplace.com

**Market category / positioning**
- Employee Experience (EX) platform focused on:
  - Employee engagement (employee listening)
  - Performance management
  - Employee development
- Delivered as **three modular but connected packages**: Engagement, Performance, Development
- Not positioned as:
  - A full HCM
  - Payroll
  - Core HRIS

**Primary buyers**
- CHROs and Heads of People
- VPs of HR / People Operations
- Executives accountable for engagement, retention, and performance outcomes

**Primary end users**
- HR Administrators (HR Managers, HRBPs)
- Managers
- Employees

**Core problems QW exists to solve**
- Help organizations understand employee sentiment at scale **without violating trust or confidentiality**
- Enable managers to build stronger teams through **structured performance and development workflows**
- Turn fragmented people data into **actionable insights**, not static reports
- Help leaders decide **where to coach, promote, retain, or intervene** using connected data and AI insights

## SECTION 2: PRODUCT PACKAGES (WHAT QW SELLS)

QW delivers value through **three modular but intentionally connected packages**.  
Each package provides standalone value, but **value increases materially when packages are combined and connected**.

### Package: Engagement
**Features included**
- Engagement surveys - Organization-wide engagement surveys
- Pulse surveys - Easily launch and analyze pulse surveys for timely topical feedback.
- Lifecycle surveys - Our lifecycle survey software simplifies and automates milestone-based data collection, allowing you to continuously collect, understand, and act on employee feedback.
- Action planning - Streamline engagement survey action planning with simple, expert-informed, AI-driven solutions. Help every leader and team transform survey results into impactful action without complexity.
- Retention Radar - Retention Radar uses AI and predictive analytics to analyze employee feedback, identify risk, and advise you on retention strategies.



**Strategic role**
- The **listening foundation** of the platform
- Generates trusted engagement signals that inform performance, development, and leadership action

### Package: Performance
**Features included**
- Performance Reviews — flexible, template-driven reviews focused on reflection and action
- Goals — aligned goal creation, tracking, and progress visibility
- 1-on-1s — structured, recurring manager–employee conversations
- Feedback — continuous, multi-directional feedback
- Recognition — real-time recognition tied to values and outcomes

**Strategic role**
- Translates insight into **day-to-day managerial action**
- Creates rhythm, clarity, and accountability at the team level

### Package: Development
**Features included**
- Growth — competencies, skills, and development planning
- Talent Reviews — structured talent calibration (performance, growth, retention risk)
- Succession Planning — identifying and preparing future leaders

**Strategic role**
- Supports **long-term team and leadership strength**
- Uses engagement and performance signals to inform talent decisions

## SECTION 3: PACKAGING & PLATFORM STRATEGY

**Packaging model**
- Products are sold as modular packages:
  - Engagement
  - Performance
  - Development
- Customers may adopt one, two, or all three packages

### 2+ Package Strategy
QW intentionally encourages customers to adopt **two or more packages** because:
- Platform value compounds as data and workflows are connected
- Customers with multiple packages are significantly more likely to retain
- Single-package, “check-the-box” use cases are not strategically aligned

**CONFIDENTIAL (internal context; do not quote externally): retention evidence**
- After two years:
  - ~73% retention for one-package customers
  - ~94% retention for three-package (platform) customers

**Implication for competitive intelligence**
Evaluate competitors not only on feature depth, but on:
- Ability to connect engagement, performance, and development
- Strength of cross-package workflows and shared data
- Whether the vendor behaves as a **platform** vs. a collection of point solutions

## SECTION 4: STRATEGIC POSITIONING & PHILOSOPHY

### Mission
**Empower every team to thrive.**

Thriving teams exhibit four conditions:
1. **Aligned** — Goals connect to organizational priorities
2. **Empowered** — Employees have a voice and can enact change
3. **Growing** — Managers support learning and development
4. **Valued** — Recognition and feedback are timely and meaningful

### Vision
**Unlock people insights through one connected experience.**

Strategic belief:
- Standalone features provide value
- Connected features deliver **multiplicative value**
- AI enables insights that are:
  - Proactive (not just descriptive)
  - Pattern-based (not anecdotal)
  - Action-oriented (embedded in workflows)

QW is evolving from a survey-first company into a **connected, AI-enabled EX platform** that helps customers build measurably stronger teams.

### Core strategic beliefs (CI framing)
- Surveys alone do not build thriving teams
- Managers are the primary leverage point for change
- Trust and confidentiality are non-negotiable
- Insights only matter if they lead to action
- AI should simplify leadership work, not replace judgment
